## Placeholders (required first)

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `item_placeholder.png` | Shown when a full illustration is missing |
| `icon_placeholder.png` | Shown when a badge icon is missing |

## Naming Convention

- Full illustration: `item_<id>.png`
- Badge icon: `icon_<id>.png`
- Recommended formats: PNG with transparency

## Art Style Notes

Post-apocalyptic wasteland aesthetic — weathered, desaturated, gritty.
Icons should read clearly at 32×32 (simple silhouette, high contrast).
Illustrations can have more detail but should still feel dark/worn.
